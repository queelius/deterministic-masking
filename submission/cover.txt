Dear Editor,

Please find enclosed our manuscript "Information Recovery under Deterministic Masking in
Exponential Series Systems" for consideration in the Journal of Statistical Planning and
Inference.

We show that for exponential series systems, a deterministic masking mechanism recovers the
complete-identification Fisher information matrix regardless of candidate set cardinality.
This result overturns the conventional assumption that larger candidate sets necessarily
degrade estimation precision, revealing that information loss is a property of the masking
mechanism's structure, not its cardinality. The result is verified via Monte Carlo
simulation.

The manuscript has not been published or submitted elsewhere. The author has no conflicts of
interest to declare.
